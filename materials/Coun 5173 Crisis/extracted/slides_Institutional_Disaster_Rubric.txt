Summary and Information
about Event
10 pts
10 – Presentation includes thorough coverage of the topic with
accurate representation of content. Evaluates the crisis event using
one of the identified crisis models in the text. Considers the
counselor’s role in working with victims of the crisis event. Uses
appropriate media examples to highlight institutional responses
and/or provide case study examples
5– Presentation is missing pieces of relevant information about the
topic that are conducive to understanding the topic.
0– Presentation is missing significant content about the topic.
Summary is evident that the topic was minimally covered or
misinterpreted.
PowerPoint
Presentation/Visual
10 pts
10 – PowerPoint/Visual is easy to read and follow, includes accurate
content of the topic and maintains attention throughout the
presentation.
5 – PowerPoint/Visual is unclear and difficult to follow but presents
appropriate content of the topic.
0 – PowerPoint/Visual is difficult to read and follow and does not
include content that is conducive to the understanding of the topic.
Media/Case Study
5 pts
5 – Relevant and applicable. Presented within time limits.
3 – Relevant and applicable. Took too much time in the presentation
0 – Not relevant or applicable in relation to the assigned topic
Overall Presentation
10 pts
10 – Overall presentation stimulates and engages peer. Presenters
conduct the presentation in a professional manner.
5 – Presentation includes appropriate content but does not stimulate
learning of peers or maintain interest. Presenters did not conduct
themselves in a professional manner or seemed unprepared to
discuss certain parts of their presentation.
0 – Presentation overall does not stimulate learning, presenters were
not prepared for this presentation, and/or presenters did not conduct
the presentations in a professional manner
Time of presentation
5 pts
5- Group presented within time frame (18 minutes to 23 minutes)
3- Presentation was under 18 minutes or over 24 minutes
0- Presentation was less than 15 minutes
