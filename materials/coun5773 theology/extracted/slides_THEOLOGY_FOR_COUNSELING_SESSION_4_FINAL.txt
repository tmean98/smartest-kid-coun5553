BROKEN
A broken image is about a broken relationship. 
Fallen humanity is engaged in the fateful turn to self-sufficiency. 
Humanity usurps from God the role of judge of wisdom and truth. 
As a result, there is blaming, shaming, and a propensity to do what is right in our own eyes. 
The capacity for connecting with God is there, but the individual will struggle for independence and reject God as Father and Jesus as Lord. 
The fear of losing control of one’s self-determination and self-fulfillment, which are illusions, begins a subtle process of developing defense mechanisms that create a self-made prison. 
From this lonely place, one pursues all the vices to nurse the loss, the emptiness, and the pain.
