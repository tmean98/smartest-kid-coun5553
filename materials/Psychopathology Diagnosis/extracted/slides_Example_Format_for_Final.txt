Example Format for Final 
You may use narrative or bullets.  Regardless of how you approach the final, be thorough. 
You may use headings to provide structure and clarity.  Consider the following headings.
Description of the client
This will only be a basic description of general demographic information.  You do not need to provide a full summary of the case.
Description of symptoms
In this section you will want to clearly identify the client’s symptoms that meet the diagnostic criteria.  Be sure make the clear connection with the criteria and to address all diagnostic criteria. 
Differential Diagnosis
Address all steps of differential diagnosis, 1-6.  In your addressing of step 4- primary disorder, be sure to articulate disorders you have considered and ruled out.  Furthermore, be sure to describe the symptom(s) that are different or absent that cause you to rule out the disorder. 
Treatment Plan
This section will be a very brief description of the evidence-based treatment you would offer based on the diagnosis you have identified.  You will provide a brief statement of rationale for choosing the approach.  Note: this is not a detailed, step-by-step treatment plan. You only need to identify the approach and provide the statement for why you have selected it.
